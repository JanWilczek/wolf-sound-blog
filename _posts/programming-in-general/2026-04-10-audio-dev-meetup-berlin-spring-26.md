The Audio Developer Meetup Berlin has new dates coming up!

If you're into audio programming, DSP, or music technology, this is one of the best ways to meet like-minded people in person.

Here are the upcoming meetup dates:

- May 6, 2026
- June 2, 2026
- July 1, 2026

All meetups take place at Ableton AG, Schoenhauser Allee 6-7, Berlin and start at 6 PM.

The meetup is free to attend, but you need to register on [the meetup's page](https://www.meetup.com/en-EN/audio-developer-meetup-berlin/).

As a reminder, Audio Developer Meetup Berlin is organized by Rachel Susser, [Fabian Renn-Giles]({% post_url collections.posts, '2024-05-20-fabian-renn-giles.md' %}), and a group of dedicated volunteers. Thank you for keeping this going!

Enjoy the talks and the post-meetup pizza 🍕

Learn my story on a game programming podcast!

At the beginning of 2026, I was interviewed on the Wookash Podcast, a podcast on game programming hosted by Łukasz Ściga (another Polish guy, yay!). Since, the podcast features many well-known programmers, like Jonathan Blow (the creator of the Jai programming language), I feel humbled and happy to have been invited!
